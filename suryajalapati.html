Challenges.


Addressing Indonesia's high maritime transport carbon emissions of 9.6 million tons per year in 2022 is crucial. We're also tackling the challenge of volatile diesel fuel prices amidst global crises and geopolitical instability. Moreover, the limitations of space, capacity, and intermittency are hindering the effective deployment of solar PV for ships. Our objective is to revolutionize maritime energy. While initial solar PV projects generated only 4% of non-critical component electricity due to intermittency, our hydrogen battery pilot project will boost renewable energy capacity by up to 20%, leading the way towards cleaner and more efficient maritime transportation.
